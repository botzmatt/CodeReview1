    About Me


    I was born in Flint, Michigan. I am the youngest of three boys. I moved out to Portland because the declining economy in Michigan did not show itself as revitalizing after 2008. I used to come out to Portland almost every summer to visit family, and I grew to love the area pretty quickly. I work as an external vendor for Microsoft as a Quality Engineer, testing video games. I have a team of 9 testers that I manage and direct in their everyday testing. We work on the game Killer Instinct; a remake of a 1990's classic fighting game.
